## 📈 Expected Client Questions & Answers

### Q: "How is this different from existing platforms?"
**A**: "We focus on the complete journey - not just hospital listings. Our assessment system intelligently matches patients with doctors & hospitals. We handle medical + accommodations + tourism, not just medical. And we focus specifically on Yogyakarta with deep relationships."

### Q: "How do you make money?"
**A**: "Commission on bookings - typically 15-20% from hospital/hotel partners. We could also charge patients small service fee. The volume model (lots of patients) makes it very profitable."

### Q: "When can this go live?"
**A**: "The frontend is done (you're seeing it now). We need backend API development (2-4 weeks), payment integration (1 week), then deployment (1 week). So 4-6 weeks total to MVP launch."

### Q: "Can we customize the doctor list?"
**A**: "Absolutely. This shows demo data, but the backend will pull real doctor data from our partner hospitals. Easy to update/add doctors anytime."

### Q: "What about data security?"
**A**: "Patient data encrypted in transit & at rest, HTTPS only, compliance-ready for health data regulations. All implemented in the backend development phase."

### Q: "How do you handle international payments?"
**A**: "We integrate with Midtrans or Xendit (Indonesian payment gateways with international card support). Multi-currency support. Full PCI compliance."

### Q: "What's the tech stack?"
**A**: "Frontend: React + TypeScript + Tailwind (modern, maintainable). Backend: Node.js or Python (we recommend Node for speed). Database: PostgreSQL. Hosting: AWS or equivalent. Cloud-native, scalable architecture."
